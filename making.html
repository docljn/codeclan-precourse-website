Jamaica Ginger Cake

Source: Mortgage Free in Three

Ingredients

  • 125g golden syrup
  • 60g margarine
  • 25g self raising flour
  • 100g plain flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 0.5 tsp mixed spice
  • 0.25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 125ml full fat milk
  • one egg

Method

  1. In a pan, melt the golden syrup and margarine.
  2. In a bowl, sift the dry ingredients together (or mix well with a fork if you can't be bothered with sieves).
  3. Whisk the egg into the milk, and pour into the dry ingredients. Mix well.
  4. Add the melted syrup and margarine, and beat until combined.
  5. Pour into a lined 2lb loaf tin and bake for 55 mins at 170C (it's done when a skewer comes out clean).
  6. Serve plain, with butter, with custard, with cream, or with ice cream.
