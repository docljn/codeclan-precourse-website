How it all began

In May 2017 I went along to a half-day taster session at CodeClan Edinburgh - more out of curiousity than anything else. I'd lived with a coder before, and had never been able to understand what was so compelling that it overrode the need for food and sleep. Now was my chance to find out.

Four hours (and some rather tasty sandwiches) later, I had the beginnings of an idea. The challenge of finding a reliable solution to a problem had always been something I enjoyed. I knew I wanted to find out more.

The next step was CodeBar where my tutors guided me through Object-Oriented Ruby and Sinatra. I started to trawl the internet for tutorials, and my Evernote list of "Things to Learn" got progressively longer. Then I went on holiday for two weeks, couldn't take my laptop, and got distinctly twitchy...

I'm about to start the CodeClan 16-week software development course - slightly daunted by the prospect but overall delighted to have the opportunity to learn so much so quickly.

Junior Developer role: here I come!
